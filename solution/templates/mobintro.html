Solvent

I Want to...

  • make it as easy as possible for people to share their ideas, and as enjoyable as possible for others to browse and discuss them.

  • create a community for people who like to build things, ask questions, and explore the possibilities around them.

  • help people see the world differently by exposing them to new points of view and encouraging them to think about how they could change it.

How it Works

  1. Organize your idea into its basic parts. This could be the context around your idea, existing related solutions, or the details of how your idea works.

  2. Share your idea, and get feedback from other users in the comments. Use this feedback to refine your idea. Rinse and repeat.

  3. Through this process, you can develop a following, find people who want to work on the idea, and recieve fedback on how to take your idea to reality.

You can Help by...

  • checking out the prototype I've put together and using it to give me feedback.

  • showing Solvent to your friends and spreading the word.

  • helping me find people who want to make Solvent a reality.

  • asking hard questions, building cool things, and imagining the world as it could be.
