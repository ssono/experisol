The Game

What can a group of passionate people imagine when they are not bounded by deadlines, resources, or doubt? What are the impossibilities they can envision together, and can those visions be transformed into reality? In order to answer these questions, I want to build Solvent: a collaborative brainstorming platform where people can propose, discuss, and improve ideas. I’ve found that exploring the ideas is both stimulating and rewarding because it lets us see how fluid the status quo really is. I want to give people the space to look past what exists today and imagine all of the possibilities that could be, and then, if they so choose, bring one to life.

This sounds like a great deal for the people getting feedback on their ideas, but what does everyone else get out of this? The short answer is nothing… at least nothing tangible. If you’re curious, you can learn about the perspectives and ideas of your peers. If you’re magnanimous, you can mentor others and help them bring their visions to life. If you’re bored, you can explore outlandish hypotheticals and talk to people who share your passion. Solvent is not meant to be a factory that takes in ideas and spits out business plans, it is meant to be a multiplayer sandbox mode. My hope is that by creating an environment of playful creativity, I can help grow a community of people who want to build new things. Here’s where you come in. Solvent is about collaborating to build things, so in that spirit, Solvent will be built by you, for you.
